We have a new brand – Strongpoint

I’ve got some exciting news that I’d like to share with you: FLODocs is rebranding – as of September 12 – to Strongpoint.

Why the change?

FLODocs was a great brand when we launched – it captured the idea that great businesses need to have great documentation. But automatic documentation was just a means to an end: helping our customers get more out of NetSuite and Salesforce stress-free. We did that by adding capabilities like ground-breaking change management, compliance reporting, and financial controls.

Our new name, Strongpoint, is about our future. It’s a play on words. To the military, a “strongpoint” is a key defensive position, and to the rest of us it’s what we’re really good at. Our software intelligently and automatically defends your systems, helping your team to be great at change management – hence Strongpoint!

The future of our company brings our three products – documentation, change management, and compliance – together as a complete continuous audit suite. A closed loop system to help our customers move quickly while being safe and compliant.

You work with a range of businesses - from SMB to Enterprise - and not every company needs a full time system administrator. With our new Managed Services Licence, you are able deploy Strongpoint as part of your monthly SysAdmin offering for these customers.

As a result of this new program, your team gets to leverage Strongpoint’s tools for troubleshooting, support and new project scoping, and your customer has peace-of-mind knowing that changes can be made safer and faster, with a clear audit trail and a versioned backup of each object, just in case.
