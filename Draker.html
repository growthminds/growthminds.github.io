Introducing the Knowledge Hub

- a helping hand for LandLords

You hardly need us to tell you that being a landlord can be a challenging business. That’s why our priority has always been to try and go above and beyond for our clients and to lend a helping hand.


Since our launch in 2010, we have always been happy to share our experiences of the industry with our clients. As lettings specialists, we have had the opportunity to become experts in what we do, and it’s nice to be able to give back.


Now we want to go a step further.


That is why we are launching the Draker Lettings Knowledge Hub – an online resource that will offer vital advice from in-house and independent experts into all aspects of being a landlord.


We’ll be keeping our Knowledge Hub regularly updated with relevant and timely articles as part of a broader series of activities that will include exclusive, invitation-only, in-branch events with guest speakers and a few drinks.


The Knowledge Hub will cover everything from getting your property ready to re-let, navigating a safe path through legislation changes, to setting the parameters for a healthy landlord-tenant relationship, and everything in between.


With the rental landscape continuing to shift, providing fresh challenges and opportunities, the Knowledge Hub will offer a helping hand to those who may need some helpful advice in order to get the best possible results.


It’s a natural progression for us here at Draker. We are trusted by our clients to let out well over 600 high-quality properties each year across Belgravia, Chelsea, Fulham, Knightsbridge, Pimlico and the surrounding areas, so we are well aware of the challenges that can face landlords.


In repaying that trust, we have always endeavoured to go the extra mile by matching up high-quality properties with reliable tenants at the best possible market rate.


Now we are going this next step further to help our clients overcome the countless potential issues that do require attention over time.

Please do check in and let us know how you are finding it. We are excited to hear your thoughts!
